Online Classroom


Find the best classroom that satisfies all your needs where you can interact directly with a teacher, work on and view assignments, grades, and lessons.


Instructions

You can access all of the features by clicking the menu icon at the top left of the screen. The options for the features include the following:


Classrooms

Here you can view and search for any of the classrooms already posted by teachers. You can apply to join a classroom and any of the teachers in the classroom are able to accept your request


Lessons

Here you can view a list of all the lessons in the classroom. If you can't view any of the lessons, please contact the teacher and ask for help


Announcements

Classroom announcements will be posted here for everyone to see. These may include exams, assignments, due dates, changes in schedule, or any important events coming up for the class.


Assignments

Assignments will be posted here for students to read over and respond to. Submissions should consist of a title and body/content


Grades

Grades for exams and assignments will be posted for students to go over in order to keep track of their progress


Members

A list of all members in the classroom, including teachers and students
