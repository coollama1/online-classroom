Online Classroom


Create a classroom for any subject you need and students will be able to join, participate, and interact in the virtual classroom environment!


Instructions

Using the options at the top, you have the option to create new classes or manage ones that you have already created. Afterwards you can access the following menu options:


Lessons

Here you can view a list of all the lessons in the classroom. As a teacher in the classroom, you also have the option to post new lessons for everyone to see


Announcements

Classroom announcements will be posted here for everyone to see. These may include exams, assignments, due dates, changes in schedule, or any important events coming up for the class.


Assignments

Assignments will be posted here for students to read over and respond to. Teachers will be able to see a list of all the assignments and create new ones if necessary


Grades

Grades for exams and assignments will be posted for students to go over in order to keep track of their progress


Members

A list of all members in the classroom, including teachers and students
